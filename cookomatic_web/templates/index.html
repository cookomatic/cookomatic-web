ABOUT COOKOMATIC

The Cookomatic Team

Ryan Hefner, Chris Elias, Alexa Ham, Vanessa Frost

The Project

The goal was to create a cooking app for iOS and Android phones that will allow the user to input recipes and have the app schedule steps for the user to complete in a way that the dishes will all complete at the same time.

Challenges

Getting the recipes from websites turned out to be a legal issue. We couldn't automatically scrape recipe sites for dish information without opening ourselves up to a potential lawsuit.

Business Plan

Originally we toyed with the idea of having users pay a monthly subscription fee and unlock extra functionality for the application. We really didn't want to use advertising revenue for our application, especially considering most recipes are time-sensitive and users can't be expected to watch a thirty-second video every few minutes.

The Scheduling Algorithm

The scheduler will take steps from each dish and order them so that the user can complete each step in order and have each dish finish cooking at the same time. It relies on the time it takes to complete a step associated with each step, and orders them accordingly.

Testing & Requirements

We had a LOT of requirements for this app, and that helped out really well for writing unit tests. For usability testing, we were fortunate enough to have several volunteers in our class, as well as from prospective students during NCSU's Engineering Open House.
